FALL LEADERSHIP CONFERENCE 2017
@ UW SEATTLE

This was our first chapter-wide event and it was a blast! We had our officer team attend along with a few members and our advisor Jeff Stride. The boat-building competition offered a great chance for us to begin bonding as a team.

WA STATE CONFERENCE 2018
@ DOUBLE TREE SEATAC

Attending our first ever State Conference was truly an unforgettable experience. Members had the opportunity to communicate and connect with their peers from across the state of Washington, as well as with industry professionals. NCHS TSA also had more than half of our members place top 5 in their events!

Congratulations to Abi Chandru, Aneesh Karpoor, Vrishab Satish Kumar, Brittany Do, Rishabh Jain, Brandon Stride, Andrew Mazzawi, Carson Petrie and Gage Bockenstedt!
